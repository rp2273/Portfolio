Netflix Clone

A "Netflix clone" is a web or mobile application that emulates key features of the popular streaming service Netflix. It typically includes user registration, a catalog of movies and TV shows, video streaming, user profiles, personalized recommendations, watchlists, user ratings and reviews, subscription plans, and responsive design. Developers often create Netflix clones for learning, or legitimate businesses,provided they have the necessary content licensing agreements and compliance with copyright laws.

TO DO LIST

A to-do list is a simple organizational tool that helps individuals track and manage tasks and activities. Users can add, edit, and prioritize tasks, making it an effective way to stay organized and ensure that important activities are not forgotten. To-do lists are commonly used in both digital and paper formats, and they are invaluable for personal productivity, project management, and time management.
